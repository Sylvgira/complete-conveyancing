Why Choose a Licensed Conveyancer?

Conveyancers are experts in conducting the legal arrangements required to transfer property and to help clients with property-related issues. In Victoria, conveyancers are required to be licensed and insured — that guarantees our work.

Conveyancers are invaluable in providing clients with independent and accurate advice on all things property-related. We regularly work together with bankers, mortgage brokers, real estate agents, property managers, owners corporations, buyers advocates, and family lawyers to ensure every transaction runs smoothly.

Whether you're buying or selling anywhere in Victoria, our dedicated team handles all end-to-end conveyancing requirements with meticulous attention to detail. We offer comprehensive advice, transparent communication, and competitive rates.

A licensed conveyancer manages the legal transfer of property ownership. We prepare and review contracts, conduct title searches, liaise with banks and real estate agents, calculate adjustments for rates and taxes, and ensure settlement runs smoothly — so you can focus on your move.

A standard residential conveyance typically takes 4–6 weeks from contract signing to settlement. The exact timeline depends on finance approvals, building and pest inspections, and the settlement date agreed in the contract. We keep you updated at every stage.
A Section 32 (Vendor's Statement) is a legal document that discloses key information about a property — zoning, easements, rates, building permits, and any restrictions.
