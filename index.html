Frequently Asked Questions

A licensed conveyancer manages the legal transfer of property ownership. We prepare and review contracts, conduct title searches, liaise with banks and real estate agents, calculate adjustments for rates and taxes, and ensure settlement runs smoothly — so you can focus on your move.
We offer transparent fixed-fee pricing tailored to your transaction. Costs depend on the property type and complexity, but you'll always receive a clear quote upfront — no surprises, no hidden fees. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote specific to your situation.
A standard residential conveyance typically takes 4–6 weeks from contract signing to settlement. The exact timeline depends on finance approvals, building and pest inspections, and the settlement date agreed in the contract. We keep you updated at every stage.
A Section 32 (Vendor's Statement) is a legal document that discloses key information about a property — zoning, easements, rates, building permits, and any restrictions. It's critical to review before signing a contract. We offer a free 24-hour review of your Section 32 and contract to ensure you're fully informed.
